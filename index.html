About Me

A creative mind with a security-first approach.

I'm an international student from Nepal, currently pursuing my degree in Cybersecurity. Before moving here, I spent over a year working as a professional Graphic Designer for an educational consultancy.

This dual background gives me a unique perspective. My design experience, using tools like Adobe Illustrator, Photoshop, and Canva, taught me how to communicate ideas visually and create engaging user experiences. I designed everything from promotional flyers and brochures to official company branding materials like ID and visiting cards.

Now, my passion for cybersecurity drives me to protect the very digital platforms I love to create. I believe that good design and strong security are not mutually exclusive—they are essential partners in building trust and functionality in the modern web.
